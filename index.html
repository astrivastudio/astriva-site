Philosophy

Not templates.
Systems.

Most Shopify stores are built to display products. Astriva builds systems to close decisions.

The difference is structural. The buyer journey has specific failure points — on the product page, in collections, at navigation, at cart. Each one breaks at a structural level, not a visual one.

Every system we build targets one failure point precisely. Not a full redesign. Not a theme. A controlled layer that resolves a specific break in the decision sequence.
